Computer Science

Technology is changing—and it’s changing the way we do business. With efficiency and excellent customer service and experience at the core, today’s businesses need tech employees who are skilled communicators and problem solvers, with knowledge around the many facets of technology solutions, such as programming, networking, databases, development, web design, and cyber security. With a Bachelor of Computer Science from Trevecca, you’ll be prepared to step into the business world and become a key player in organizational success. With an emphasis on user experience and web design, as well as front and back end development, this degree will prepare students to lead projects, develop software and business domains and collaborate with others to develop better technology for businesses. By learning to design solutions, this degree will prepare students to be problem solvers, as well as innovators.

You will be equiped to:

  • Analyze business and technical needs that lead to creative and meaningful hardware and software solutions through effective and efficient project managememnt strategies
  • Develop and develop complex algorithms and data structures for web-based client/server networked solutions and embedded systems in disparate and complex environments
  • Demonstrate skills in software engineering or game design through the development and presentation of a final project and porfolio

Required Minor

  • Web Design and Development
  • Software Engineering
  • Rich Media Design
  • Social Media and Digital Marketing
  • UX Design
  • Game Design and Development
  • Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics
