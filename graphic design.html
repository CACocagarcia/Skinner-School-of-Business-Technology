Graphic Design

The Digital Graphic Design Bachelor of Science in Information Technology is designed to be a creative outlet in which you can express yourself through technology. It will provide you with the tools you need to create an immersive multimedia experience for the users of your webpages. You will learn coding, programming, typesetting, and conceptual thinking that will assist you in creating exceptional webpages.

You will be equiped to:

  • Analyze buisiness and technical needs that lead to creative and meaningful grapahic desing anad digital marketing solutions through efficient and effective project management strategies
  • Design and develop diitial marketing automation, UX/UI desing, and rich media solutions for disparate and complex businesses environments
  • Demonstrate skills in web design, UX/UI design, and rich media design through the development and presentation of a final project and portfolio

Required Minor

  • Web Design and Development
  • Software Engineering
  • Rich Media Design
  • Social Media and Digital Marketing
  • UX Design
  • Game Design and Development
  • Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics
