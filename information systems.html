Information Systems

It is predicted that information technology will rank as one of the top career opportunity areas in both Tennessee and the nation in the coming years. The Specialized Computing concentration of the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology is designed to help you be an asset in this growing career field.
With the help of state-of-the-art software and technologies, Trevecca University is able to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of this exciting field. While a student at Trevecca, you will learn to code, program, design, and utilize technology in a way that positively impacts culture and the human experience.

You will be equiped to:

  • Analyze business and technical needs that lead to creative and meaningful holistic information systems and solutions through effective and efficient project management strategies
  • Design information systems for global e-business from a foundational understanding of hardware, software, databases, and networking to improve and apply business intelligence
  • Demonstrate skills in IT architecture and enterprise applications through the development and presentation of a final project and portfolio

Required Minor

  • Web Design and Development
  • Software Engineering
  • Rich Media Design
  • Social Media and Digital Marketing
  • UX Design
  • Game Design and Development
  • Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics
