About Us

Welcome to the Department of Information Technology. With more than four decades of industry experience, our faculty is committed to delivering the highest standard of education with the greatest attention to detail and student satisfaction in a Christ-centered environment. Trevecca offers many exciting degrees in the areas of technology. For students seeking to advance their career in IT management, we offer an MBA with courses focusing on skills needed to manage today’s complex IT environments. For students looking for a traditional four-year University experience, Trevecca offers a wide range of degrees, including information technology, Web development, specialized computing, and graphic design and multimedia. In addition, for those working adults seeking to finish their bachelor’s degree, Trevecca offers accelerated degree- completion programs in both computer information technology and healthcare information technology.
